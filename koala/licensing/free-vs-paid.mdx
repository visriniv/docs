## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try before buying?

Yes! The extension includes free mode to test basic functionality. Trial licenses may be available in your region.

### What happens when license expires?

The extension reverts to free mode. All your data and settings are preserved.

### Can I upgrade mid-year?

Yes, licenses are pro-rated. You only pay for remaining months.

### Is the license per-user or per-machine?

Licenses are per-user. You can use on multiple machines you personally use.
