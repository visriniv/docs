Where the dashboard gives you the headline view, the **cost explorer** lets you get down to the individual FOCUS line items and trace any number back to its source.

## Filter and browse

Narrow the data with filters — date range, provider, service category, subscription/tenancy, region, and resource group — then browse the matching **line-item records** (resource name and ID, compartment, cost). Because every figure comes from SQL over your ingested data, what you see always reconciles back to the source.

## Export and clean up

- **Export** the current view to **CSV** for offline analysis or sharing.
- **Purge** ingested data for a scope when you need to clear or re-load it (a confirmation step guards against accidents).

<Tip>
  To break spend down by an application tag or split shared costs, use the [Allocation](/finops/allocation) page — that's where tag pivots and split allocation live.
</Tip>
