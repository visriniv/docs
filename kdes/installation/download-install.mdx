---
title: "Download & Install"
description: "How to install Koala Data Explorer Studio on your machine"
---

## 1. Get the Installer

1. Visit the [Koala download portal](https://www.btsscorp.com/koala-data-explorer/) (the same link appears in your license email and Freshdesk articles).
2. Choose the installer for your OS:
   - **macOS:** `.dmg`
   - **Windows:** `
   .msi`

## 2. Install

### macOS

1. Open the `.dmg`.
2. Drag **Koala Data Explorer Studio** into `Applications`.
3. On first launch, right-click > **Open** to bypass Gatekeeper if prompted.

### Windows

1. Run the `Koala Data Explorer.msi` you downloaded.
2. Approve the BTSS Corp publisher prompt (signed binary).
3. Follow the wizard; a Start Menu entry is created automatically.

## 3. Sign In & Update

- Launch the app and sign in with your Koala credentials.
- The in-app banner notifies you when a newer build is available; click **Restart & Update** to stay current.
